Airstory Researcher (Chrome Extension)

How to install:

  1. Click on the link above to download AirstoryResearcher.crx. Installation will be blocked, that is to be expected, the CRX will still be downloaded to your Downloads folder.
  2. In the top right of the Chrome browser, click the Chrome Menu . Select "Settings" then click on "Extensions"
  3. Drag and drop the CRX file onto the Chrome Extensions page to install it. You'll be prompted to confirm installation of the extension, just as if you had installed it from the Chrome Web Store.
